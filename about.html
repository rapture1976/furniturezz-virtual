About Furniturezz

Welcome to Furniturezz! Thousands of items of furniture end up in land-fill every year that could be reused, repaired or recycled, and it is Furniturezz mission to reduce this. Our website helps you re-home your unwanted furniture and household goods to buyers in your local area. Reduce waste and precious resources, and furnish your home with quality items for free!
